Tourism and the Rise of Cases

Oregon relies heavily on tourism as a means of income. Visitors choose our state as a travel destination every year from all over the country. Portland alone saw over 11 million visitors in 2021.

It’s not surprising, given the natural beauty and way of life offered here. However, the number of visitors is only continuing to grow, and despite the initial setbacks of the pandemic, businesses are feeling the pressure from it. Under normal circumstances, tourists would be a welcome addition to the income needed by most restaurants and small shops, but most are feeling the pressure of keeping their employees safe and their workplaces sanitized.
